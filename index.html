My Favourite Dish Chilli Chicken

Chilli chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish of chicken of Hakka Chinese heritage. In India, this may include a variety of dry chicken preparations. Though mainly boneless chicken is used in this dish, some people also use bone-in chicken too.

Chilli chicken comes in several variations with differences in spices & seasoning, as well as its "sauce" & "dry" variants with difference in their quantity of sauce or gravy. The South Asian contributions of this dish are in the spices used, while the Hakka contributions include the sweet & savoury flavours along with the Chinese cooking techniques used in its preparation.
